General Questions

Is There a Reservation Without Credit Card?

You can book our hotel without a credit card and cancel up to 24 hours before.

Is There a Reservation Opportunity Outside Your Website?

Since our hotel is one of the most respected and quality hotels in Istanbul, it is listed on reputable and reliable hotel sites such as Booking.com, etstur.com, Odamax.com, Sorgukal.com, trip.com, agoda.com. If you wish, you can also make reservations from these sites.

What are Paid Services?

You can get many services from our hotel, from ironing of your clothes to dry cleaning, from car rental to shuttle services, for a fee. Hotel Bossuite Business will organize the service so that you can pay for these services at the exit of the hotel.

Is there WİFİ facility?

Unlimited wifi is offered to all our guests in all areas of our hotel. You do not pay any fees for this service.
